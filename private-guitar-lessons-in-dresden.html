Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What level do I need to start lessons?

Lessons are open to all levels, from complete beginners to advanced players.

Can I really learn to play at any age?

Yes. In my experience, my adult students have been showing the fastest growth as they usually can organize themselves in finding regular time to practice.

Are lessons available for children?

Absolutely! I have been working with kids for more than 20 years now. I usually start with children of 5 years old.

What styles of music do you teach?

My teaching is rooted in acoustic fingerstyle as I believe it is an important base for playing any instrument and having a direct connection with it. But I also love teaching electric guitar (Jazz, Blues, Rock, Funk) to non-beginners.

How long are the lessons?

Lesson durations depend on your needs and availability. Typically, lessons last 30 minutes when scheduled weekly. But they also can go up to 45 or 60 minutes, depending on the age, level and availability of the student.

Do you offer online lessons?

Yes, I offer online lessons via Zoom. These lessons are very effective for my students who would need to travel a long way to Dresden-Neustadt or to the students who need to keep a flexible calendar.

What is your teaching experience and background?

I have been working with children for almost 20 years and have been teaching the guitar in public and private music schools for almost 10 years. I play the guitar for more than 30 years now and have studied classical music and jazz in Paris and the USA. My experience includes performing, composing, and working with students of all ages.

Do I need to have my own guitar?

Yes, having your own instrument is ideal for practice, but I can help you choose one if needed.

How long does it take to learn guitar?

It depends on your musical experience, your goals, and practice time! Most of my students play their first melodies within a couple of months.

Do you provide lesson materials, or should I purchase my own?

I provide lesson materials, including sheet music and exercises. However, some books or additional resources may be recommended depending on your level and interests.

What is your cancellation policy?

If you have booked a lesson and need to cancel it, please inform me at least 48 hours in advance. Cancellations made later may not be rescheduled.

How do you tailor lessons to individual goals?

I customize lessons based on your musical interests, whether itâ€™s learning songs, improvisation, theory, or specific techniques.

Is there an opportunity to perform in recitals or group settings?

Yes! I regularly organize student concerts where you can showcase your progress and gain confidence performing in front of an audience full of other students.

How do I book a lesson?

You can book directly via the website or get in touch for scheduling options.
