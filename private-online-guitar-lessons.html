Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What technology do I need for online lessons?

You'll need a device with camera/microphone (computer recommended), stable internet connection, and Zoom/Skype/WhatsApp installed. Headphones can improve audio quality. After booking, you'll receive a detailed Setup Guide PDF with all requirements.

How is the audio quality for guitar lessons online?

While not perfect, modern platforms handle guitar frequencies well. I use professional audio equipment on my end, and we can adjust settings to optimize your experience. The Setup Guide PDF you'll receive after booking includes audio optimization tips.

Can I still participate in concerts/events?

Yes! Online students are welcome to join our live events in Dresden when possible, or participate virtually in special online performances. Many online students choose to visit Dresden occasionally for in-person workshops or concerts.

What if I have technical issues during a lesson?

We'll troubleshoot together, and if needed, reschedule the lost time. The Setup Guide PDF you receive after booking helps prevent most common issues, and I'm always available to help with technical questions before your lesson.

Can I combine online and in-person lessons?

Absolutely! Many students find a hybrid approach works bestâ€”regular online lessons with occasional in-person sessions when you're in Dresden. This combines convenience with hands-on guidance.

Do you offer online lessons?

Yes, I offer online lessons via Zoom, Skype or WhatsApp. These lessons are very effective for students who need flexibility or live too far to travel to Dresden-Neustadt regularly.

What is your teaching experience and background?

I have been working with children for almost 20 years and have been teaching the guitar in public and private music schools for almost 10 years. Since COVID-19, I've specialized in making online lessons just as effective as in-person instruction.

What languages do you teach in?

I'm fluent in English, French, and German, and happy to conduct lessons in whichever language you're most comfortable with. Many students find it helpful to learn music terminology in their native language first before transitioning to international terms.

How long does it take to learn guitar online?

Many students progress just as quickly online as in person! It depends on your musical experience, goals, and practice time. Most play their first melodies within a couple of months.

Do you provide lesson materials, or should I purchase my own?

I provide all lesson materials digitallyâ€”sheet music, exercises, and recordings. Some books may be recommended depending on your level, but everything essential is shared via screen or PDF.

What is your cancellation policy for online lessons?

If you need to cancel, please inform me at least 24 hours in advance (more flexible than in-person lessons). Late cancellations may not be rescheduled.

How do you tailor online lessons to individual goals?

Just like in-person, I customize based on your interestsâ€”whether learning songs, improvisation, theory or techniques. We'll use screen sharing, digital annotations, and customized practice materials.

Are morning/afternoon times available?

Yes! Online lessons are typically available 9am-2pm CET, perfect for students in different time zones or with flexible schedules.

How do I book an online lesson?

Book directly via the website. After booking, you'll receive confirmation with the Setup Guide PDF and all necessary technical information to prepare for your first lesson.
