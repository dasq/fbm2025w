Who I Am

Welcome

This is me

I am a globetrotter in the field of the guitar, combining flamenco, jazz and blues with influences from Arabic and European music culture. Often compared to masters such as Andy McKee, John Butler and Rodrigo y Gabriela, my music invites you to dream of faraway lands. It takes you on a soulful journey through Asia, North Africa, Europe and beyond.

Awards

Best Live Film Scoring Performance
"It was brought to life with live music once again—and what a delight it was! In front of a full house, Farid Ben Miles won the jury prize for his score to the film Hochofen (Blast Furnace). According to the jury—composed of Annett Groh, Florian Hohmann, and Christian "Lasse" Brähler—the guitarist created the perfect mood for the film, seamlessly merging image and sound. With his calm and atmospheric melodies, Miles set the blast furnace ablaze in the Motorenhalle.”

Dresdner Schmalfilmtage 2022

Sound of Dresden - Public Price
"The instrumental guitarist from France has traveled the world and developed his own unique musical language, drawing from many sources: African, European, and American. World music in the truest sense, yet so distinctive and captivating that he has become the audience favorite.”

Sound of Dresden 2017

VIERTELtakt Winner
"With deliberately crafted rhythms, he played his way into the hearts of the audience with his “Cinematic World Fingerstyle.” Farid Ben Miles transported us with his magnificent acoustic compositions into a world where we could peacefully let our thoughts wander. From the Orient to New Zealand, from Paris to the Florence of the Elbe, from the Land of Takka-Tukka Adventures to The NeverEnding Story — there were no limits to dreams and imagination.”

ViertelTakt Dresden 2016
