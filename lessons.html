Christmas Album for Guitar and Ukulele

Student Project

In the Autumn 2024, I had the pleasure to produce a Christmas Album with all my students who were willing to participate, whatever their age or learning experience.
It reflects their musical growth, dedication, and the joy they find in playing music. Some students have been playing acoustic guitar for years, while others are just starting out. What unites them all is their passion and enthusiasm for this project.
In this collection, each student chose one to three pieces from their repertoire ‚Äì sometimes freshly learned ‚Äì and recorded them solo in front of the microphone. While most students have recorded solo arrangements of beloved Christmas classics, others explored the magic of recording together, creating duo or trio arrangements.
