Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What level do I need to start lessons?

Lessons are open to all levels, from complete beginners to advanced players.

Can I really learn to play at any age?

Yes. In my experience, my adult students have been showing the fastest growth as they usually can organize themselves in finding regular time to practice in the regularly.

Are lessons available for children?

Absolutely! I have been working with kids for more than 20 years now. I usually start with children of 5 years old.

What styles of music do you teach?

My teaching is rooted in acoustic fingerstyle as I believe it is an important base for playing any instrument and have a direct contact with it. But I also love teaching electric guitar (Jazz, Blues, Rock, Funk) to non-beginners.

Do I need to have my own guitar?

Yes, having your own instrument is ideal for practice, but I can help you choose one if needed.

How long does it take to learn guitar?

It depends on your musical experience, your goals and practice time! Though, most of my students have already played their first melodies within a couple of months.

Can I take a trial lesson before committing?

Yes, I offer trial lessons so you can see if it‚Äôs the right fit for you.

How do I book a lesson?

You can book directly via the website or get in touch for scheduling options.
